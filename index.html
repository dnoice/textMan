Instructions

Welcome to textMan, your ultimate tool for text manipulation. Follow the steps below to edit, transform, and manage your text effortlessly.

  1. Notepad: Type or paste your text into the notepad area. Use the toolbar to undo, redo, copy, or paste text. While keyboard shortcuts like Ctrl + Z and Ctrl + Y can be used, the toolbar buttons are recommended for the best experience.
  2. Change Order: Reorganize your text by sorting lines alphabetically (A to Z or Z to A), by length (shortest to longest or vice versa), or shuffle the lines randomly.
  3. Transform Text: Convert your text to lowercase, uppercase, or capitalize each word. You can also invert the text if needed.
  4. Add Prefix/Suffix: Easily add a prefix or suffix to each line of text using the input fields and buttons provided.
  5. Remove Unwanted Content: Use the available options to remove duplicates, empty lines, extra spaces, accents, numbers, punctuation, or line breaks from your text.
  6. Search and Replace: Find specific words or phrases in your text and replace them with a new term of your choice.
  7. Filter Lines: Keep or remove lines that contain a specific word or phrase, helping you refine your text efficiently.

After making your changes, simply click the corresponding button to apply the operation. You can always use the undo and redo buttons to revert or repeat your actions. Have fun manipulating your text with textMan!
